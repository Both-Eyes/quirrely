# Sentense Logo Concepts — Leucistic Squirrel

## Brand Context

| Element | Value |
|---------|-------|
| **Mascot** | Leucistic Squirrel |
| **Primary Color** | Coral #FF6B6B |
| **Secondary Color** | Teal #4ECDC4 |
| **Accent** | Yellow #FFE66D |
| **Background** | Warm Cream #FFFBF5 |
| **Typography** | Outfit (display), Nunito Sans (body) |
| **Tone** | Curious, warm, intelligent, approachable |

## Why Leucistic Squirrel Works for Sentense

- **Leucistic** = rare, unique, distinctive (like your writing voice)
- **Squirrel** = curious, gathering, collecting (like collecting words/sentences)
- **White/pale coloring** = stands out, memorable, clean
- **Intelligent eyes** = observant, analytical (like the LNCP parser)
- **Playful energy** = approachable, not intimidating

## 10 Logo Interpretations


### Concept 1: "The Listener"
**Style:** Minimal line art  
**Pose:** Squirrel sitting upright, head tilted, ear perked — as if listening to your words  
**Details:**
- Single continuous line drawing
- Leucistic white fill with subtle warm gray shadows
- Coral accent on inner ear (the listening element)
- Works at favicon size (simplified to head only)

**Wordmark:** "Sentense" with the squirrel sitting on the "S"

```
     ◠◠
    (◉ ◉)   ← tilted head, listening
     ╰┬╯
      │
```

---

### Concept 2: "The Collector"
**Style:** Geometric/modern  
**Pose:** Squirrel holding a glowing sentence/quote like an acorn  
**Details:**
- Stylized geometric shapes (circles, triangles)
- The "acorn" is actually a speech bubble or quotation mark
- Leucistic body in cream/white
- Coral glow around the collected "sentence"
- Tail forms a subtle "S" shape

**Wordmark:** Separate, clean typography below

---

### Concept 3: "The Scribe"
**Style:** Sophisticated illustration  
**Pose:** Squirrel with a tiny quill or pen, writing  
**Details:**
- More detailed, editorial illustration style
- Leucistic squirrel with pale pink eyes (authentic leucism)
- Holding a coral-colored quill
- Ink trail forms the word "Sentense"
- Premium, literary feel

**Best for:** Blog headers, about page, large formats

---

### Concept 4: "The Mark"
**Style:** Abstract symbol  
**Pose:** Squirrel silhouette merged with quotation marks  
**Details:**
- Negative space design
- Squirrel tail curves to form opening quote "
- Body forms the closing quote "
- Pure white on coral background (or inverse)
- Extremely scalable, works tiny

**Best for:** Favicon, app icon, watermark

---

### Concept 5: "The Curious"
**Style:** Playful mascot  
**Pose:** Squirrel peeking over a line of text, curious expression  
**Details:**
- Friendly, Pixar-esque style
- Big expressive eyes
- Paws resting on a "sentence" line
- Leucistic white with warm cream undertones
- Coral nose/inner ears

**Wordmark:** Text below with squirrel peeking from above

```
       ∩∩
      (◕‿◕)  ← peeking over
    ──────────
     sentense
```

---

### Concept 6: "The Acorn S"
**Style:** Typographic integration  
**Pose:** Squirrel curled around the letter "S"  
**Details:**
- The "S" in Sentense IS the squirrel
- Tail forms top curve, body forms bottom curve
- Minimalist, single stroke
- Works as standalone icon or within wordmark
- Leucistic cream color with coral "S" outline

**Best for:** Primary logo, all sizes

---

### Concept 7: "The Reader"
**Style:** Cozy illustration  
**Pose:** Squirrel curled up reading a tiny book/scroll  
**Details:**
- Warm, inviting scene
- Leucistic squirrel with fluffy tail wrapped around
- Reading glasses optional (adds personality)
- Book/scroll has visible text lines
- Teal accent on the book

**Best for:** Blog, loading states, empty states

---

### Concept 8: "The Dot"
**Style:** Ultra-minimal  
**Pose:** Squirrel as a simple dot with ears and tail  
**Details:**
- Reduced to absolute essentials: circle + 2 triangles (ears) + curved line (tail)
- Leucistic white circle
- Coral dot for eye
- Can be animated (tail wag)
- Perfect for favicon, loading spinner

```
  ∧ ∧
 ( ● )~
```

---

### Concept 9: "The Voice"
**Style:** Sound wave integration  
**Pose:** Squirrel silhouette with sound waves emanating  
**Details:**
- Squirrel profile facing right
- Sound/voice waves coming from mouth area
- Waves use the coral-to-teal gradient
- Represents "finding your voice"
- Modern, tech-forward feel

**Best for:** App icon, social cards

---

### Concept 10: "The Profile"
**Style:** Cameo/medallion  
**Pose:** Regal squirrel portrait in profile  
**Details:**
- Classic cameo style, circular frame
- Leucistic squirrel in elegant side profile
- Frame in coral or gold
- Premium, distinguished feel
- Nods to "profile" (writing profile)

**Best for:** Pro badges, premium features, certificates

---

## Recommended Primary Logo

**Concept 6: "The Acorn S"** — Most versatile

- Works at all sizes (favicon to billboard)
- Memorable and unique
- Integrates squirrel into typography naturally
- Easy to animate
- Clear connection to brand name

**Secondary/Mascot:**
**Concept 5: "The Curious"** — For personality

- Use in onboarding, empty states, error pages
- Friendly face for the brand
- Can have expressions (happy, thinking, celebrating)
