## INSIGHTS

### Who Converts Best?

**Profile Pattern:**
- **LONGFORM** and **ASSERTIVE** convert 2.5-3x better than **MINIMAL** and **HEDGED**
- Writers who invest time (long sentences) or show conviction (assertive) are more likely to pay
- Cautious writers (hedged, minimal) hesitate at paywall

**Stance Pattern:**
- **CLOSED** stance converts 2x better than **OPEN**
- Decisive people make purchasing decisions faster
- "Open" stance users are exploring, not committing

**Best Combo:** LONGFORM + CLOSED (15.63% conversion)
- These users write long, committed prose — they're invested and decisive

**Worst Combo:** MINIMAL + OPEN (2.44% conversion)
- These users write sparse, uncertain prose — hesitant at every step

### Revenue Concentration

- Top 3 profiles (ASSERTIVE, CONVERSATIONAL, DENSE) = 48% of revenue
- Top 2 stances (CLOSED, BALANCED) = 63% of revenue
- Top 10 combos = 52% of revenue

### Funnel Drop-off by Type

| Stage | Best Retention | Worst Retention |
|-------|----------------|-----------------|
| A1 → A2 | LONGFORM (85%) | MINIMAL (68%) |
| A2 → A3 | LONGFORM (73%) | HEDGED (61%) |
| Paywall → Auth | CLOSED (43%) | OPEN (35%) |
| Auth → Subscribe | CLOSED (42%) | OPEN (34%) |
