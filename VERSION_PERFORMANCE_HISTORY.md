## Key Learnings

### 1. Quick Wins Have Outsized Impact

The v1.1.0 changes (guarantee badge, progress indicator, email capture) required minimal engineering but delivered +216% conversion. Always ship quick wins first.

### 2. Rescue Low-Intent Users

The Adaptive Funnel's LOW tier rescue (+263% conversion for that segment) proved that users who seem unlikely to convert just need different messaging, not dismissal.

### 3. Viral Is a Growth Channel

Adding sharing mechanics in v1.3.0 created a new acquisition channel that delivers 26% of visitors for free. Viral coefficient of 0.36 means every 100 users generate 36 more.

### 4. Annual Shift Compounds

Across versions, the annual ratio shifted from 40% to 52.5%. This compounds into higher LTV and lower churn over time.
