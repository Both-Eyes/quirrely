## Viral Mechanics — The Multiplier

### Viral Coefficient

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Share rate | 60.9% of users share results |
| Avg reach | 150 impressions per share |
| Click-through | 5% CTR on shares |
| Qualification | 15% of clickers become users |
| **Viral K** | **0.36** |

### What This Means

For every **100 users** acquired, viral generates **36 more for free**.

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Paid/organic seed | 65,000 users |
| Viral multiplier | 1.56x |
| Viral users | 36,000+ additional |
| Total reach | 100,000+ users |

### Optimizing Viral

1. **Share card design** — Eye-catching, curiosity-inducing
2. **Share prompts** — "See how your friends write" CTAs
3. **Comparison mechanic** — "Compare with a friend" feature
4. **Results that beg sharing** — "I'm a POETIC writer with CLOSED stance"
