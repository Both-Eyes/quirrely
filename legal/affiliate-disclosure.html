Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Quirrely participates in affiliate programs with book retailers. This page explains how these programs work and how they affect you.

What This Means

When we recommend books based on your writing profile, we provide links to purchase them from local bookstores in your country. If you click these links and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

How We Choose Books

Our book recommendations are based on:

  • Your writing profile and communication style
  • Books that exemplify similar writing approaches
  • Quality and relevance to your interests

Commission rates do not influence which books we recommend. We prioritize relevance and quality.

Supporting Local Bookstores

We deliberately partner with national bookstore chains rather than global marketplaces. This supports local book retail in each of our target countries.

No Extra Cost

Purchasing through our affiliate links costs you nothing extra. The commission comes from the retailer's marketing budget, not from an increase in price.

Your Choice

You are never required to use our affiliate links. You can always search for recommended books directly at any retailer of your choice.
