# QUIRRELY PRIVACY POLICY

**Last Updated:** February 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

---

## The Short Version

- **We don't use tracking cookies.** No cookie banner needed.
- **We don't sell your data.** Ever.
- **We don't track you across the web.** We only know what you do on Quirrely.
- **Your writing is yours.** We analyze it to provide the service, not to train AI models.
- **You can delete everything.** Your data, your choice.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

| Data | Why We Collect It |
|------|-------------------|
| Email address | Account creation, login, notifications |
| Password | Account security (stored hashed, never readable) |
| Display name | Public profile (optional) |
| Writing samples | Voice analysis (the core service) |
| Payment information | Subscription billing (processed by Stripe, not stored by us) |

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

| Data | Why We Collect It |
|------|-------------------|
| Pages visited | Understand how you use Quirrely |
| Feature usage | Improve the product |
| Device type | Ensure the site works on your device |
| Country (approximate) | Show correct currency, comply with laws |

**We use Plausible Analytics**, a privacy-focused analytics tool that:
- Does **not** use cookies
- Does **not** track you across sites
- Does **not** collect personal information
- Is fully GDPR compliant

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

| Purpose | Legal Basis (GDPR) |
|---------|-------------------|
| Provide the service | Contract |
| Process payments | Contract |
| Send service emails (receipts, password resets) | Contract |
| Send optional emails (streaks, milestones) | Consent |
| Improve the product | Legitimate interest |
| Prevent fraud and abuse | Legitimate interest |
| Comply with legal obligations | Legal obligation |

---

## 3. What We DON'T Do

- ❌ Sell your data to advertisers
- ❌ Share your data with data brokers
- ❌ Use tracking cookies
- ❌ Build advertising profiles
- ❌ Track you across the web
- ❌ Use your writing to train AI models
- ❌ Read your writing (except for abuse prevention)

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We share data only with:

| Recipient | Purpose | Data Shared |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| **Stripe** | Payment processing | Email, payment method |
| **Supabase** | Database hosting | All account data (encrypted) |
| **Resend** | Email delivery | Email address, email content |
| **Plausible** | Analytics | Anonymized usage data |
| **Sentry** | Error tracking | Error reports (no personal data) |

We have data processing agreements with all providers.

---

## 5. Data Retention

| Data | Retention |
|------|-----------|
| Account data | Until you delete your account |
| Writing analyses | Until you delete your account |
| Email logs | 90 days |
| Analytics | Aggregated forever, raw events 90 days |
| Deleted accounts | Soft deleted 30 days, then permanently deleted |

---

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

| Right | How to Exercise |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Access** your data | Settings → Export data |
| **Correct** your data | Settings → Edit profile |
| **Delete** your data | Settings → Delete account |
| **Export** your data | Settings → Export data (JSON) |
| **Withdraw consent** | Email preferences or contact us |
| **Object** to processing | Contact us |
| **Lodge a complaint** | Contact your local data protection authority |

---

## 7. Data Security

We protect your data with:

- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption at rest (AES-256)
- Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
- Regular security updates
- Access controls and audit logging
- No unnecessary data collection

---

## 8. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in:

- **Canada** (primary)
- **United States** (Supabase, Stripe)
- **European Union** (some providers)

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

Quirrely is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us immediately.

---

## 10. Cookies

**We don't use tracking cookies.**

We use only essential cookies for:

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `quirrely_auth` | Keep you logged in | Session |
| `quirrely_country` | Remember your country preference | 1 year |

That's it. No analytics cookies. No advertising cookies. No third-party tracking cookies.
