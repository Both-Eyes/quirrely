## Product

**What it does:** Analyzes writing samples to reveal a user's unique "writing voice" — one of 10 profiles (Minimalist, Dense, Poetic, etc.) plus certainty stance.

**How it's different:**
- Grammarly fixes writing → Sentense reveals identity
- 16Personalities tests personality → Sentense tests communication style
- No direct competitor in "writing voice analysis"

**Technical:**
- Single-file web app (9,332 lines)
- Zero dependencies
- Static hosting ($20/mo max)
- No AI/API costs (rule-based analysis)
