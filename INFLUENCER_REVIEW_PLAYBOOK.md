>
> One quibble: the reading list felt slightly American-default. I'd love to see more CanLit recommendations. But this is a small thing.
>
> If you're a writer who's tired of being told to "simplify" and "clarify," try this. It might be the first tool that actually sees you.

**Why This Profile Works:**
- Literary credibility lends authority
- Teaching angle creates institutional spread
