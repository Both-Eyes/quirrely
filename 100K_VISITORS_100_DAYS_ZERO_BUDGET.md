## Traffic Spike Playbook

### Day 7: Product Hunt Launch

**Target:** 2,000 visitors

**Tactics:**
- Pre-launch teaser campaign
- Hunter with 1K+ followers
- Launch at 12:01 AM PT
- Engage all comments within 1 hour
- Cross-post to Twitter, LinkedIn

### Day 14: Hacker News Front Page

**Target:** 5,000 visitors

**Tactics:**
- "Show HN" post with technical angle
- Title: "Show HN: I built a writing analyzer that doesn't try to fix you"
- Post at 8 AM ET
- Respond to every comment
- Avoid self-promotion, focus on technical discussion

### Day 21: Viral Twitter Thread

**Target:** 3,000 visitors

**Tactics:**
- Thread: "I analyzed 10,000 writing samples. Here's what I learned about how different countries write."
- Lead with data/insights, not product
- Include visual comparisons (CA vs UK vs AU vs NZ)
- End with soft CTA: "Try it yourself"

### Day 28: Reddit r/writing

**Target:** 2,000 visitors

**Tactics:**
- Post genuinely helpful content first
- Share as a tool, not an ad
- Respond to questions about methodology
- Let community share their results

### Day 35: LinkedIn Viral Post

**Target:** 2,000 visitors

**Tactics:**
- Personal story format
- "I spent 6 months building a tool to understand my writing voice..."
- Professional angle: "What your writing says about you at work"
- Tag relevant connections

### Day 42: Influencer Mention

**Target:** 3,000 visitors

**Tactics:**
- Seed 10-15 writing Twitter accounts
- Offer free Pro for genuine coverage
- Let them discover + share organically
- Amplify their posts when they share
