Privacy Notice

Quirrely does not collect, store, or transmit your writing. All analysis happens in your browser. Your text never leaves your device.

We use localStorage to save anonymous session analytics (page views, button clicks) to improve the experience. No personal information is collected. Social login buttons are for future features and do not currently connect to any service.
