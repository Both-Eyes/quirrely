Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Quirrely and your writing voice.

What is Quirrely and how does it work?▼

Quirrely is a writing voice analysis tool. Paste a sample of your writing, and our LNCP engine analyzes it across 50 linguistic tokens to reveal your unique voice profile. You get a voice type (like Assertive or Conversational) and a stance (Open, Closed, Balanced, or Contradictory) that together describe how you write.

What are voice types and stances?▼

Quirrely identifies 10 voice types: Conversational, Assertive, Poetic, Analytical, Minimal, Provocative, Reflective, Technical, Narrative, and Persuasive. Each is paired with one of 4 stances — Open, Closed, Balanced, or Contradictory — describing how your writing engages with its audience.

How do I take a writing voice test?▼

From the home page, paste any sample of your writing into the text area and click Analyze. For best results, use 200+ words of natural writing — an email, blog post, or journal entry works great. You can save your results and retake the test anytime.

Is my writing data private?▼

Yes. Your writing samples and results are private by default. You can optionally enable a public profile in Settings to share your voice type, but your actual text is never shown publicly. You can clear your history or delete your account at any time.

How do I improve my writing voice?▼

Pro subscribers get access to STRETCH — targeted writing exercises designed around your specific voice profile. You can also retake the analysis with different writing samples to see how your voice shifts across contexts. Your dashboard tracks your voice evolution over time so you can see growth.

What countries is Quirrely available in?▼

Quirrely is available in Canada, the United States, the United Kingdom, Australia, and New Zealand. Our recommended reading feature links to local bookstores in each supported country.

How do I share my voice profile?▼

From your dashboard, click the share button on your voice card. You can copy a direct link, share to LinkedIn, or post to Facebook. Your public profile shows your voice type and stance — never your raw text.

Can I delete my data?▼

Yes. Go to Settings where you can clear your analysis history or delete your entire account. Deletion is permanent and removes all your data from our servers.

How do I get featured as a writer?▼

Pro and Featured tier subscribers can submit writing samples for review. If selected, your work appears on our Featured Writers page and you earn a Featured badge on your public profile. Submit from your dashboard once you have an active Pro subscription.
